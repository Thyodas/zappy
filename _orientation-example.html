Orientation

Convert player relative pos to absolute pos on the board. This function takes into account the player's orientation. NORTH x = -1 and y = 1 will take player pos x substract one and take player pos y and add 1. If player is looking EAST x will be considered as y and vice versa.

Parameters
zappyThe zappy data.
playerThe player to calculate with.
xPlayer's relative pos x.
yPlayer's relative pos y.
Returns
Absolute position on the board.
